Before the Fall


My name was Arthas Menethil, Crown Prince of Lordaeron and paladin of the Knights of the Silver Hand. I was the son of King Terenas Menethil and dear friend to Uther the Lightbringer and Jaina Proudmoore.

The Fall


I became corrupted while desperately trying to save my people from the plague and the demon Mal'Ganis. Breaking from my honor as a paladin, I purged the city of Stratholme and defeated Mal'Ganis by taking up the cursed runeblade Frostmourne. I lost my soul, becoming a death knight of the Scourge. I led to the destruction of Lordaeron, Quel'Thalas, and Dalaran.'

The Rise of The Lich King


I finally cast aside the remaining fragment of my humanity and let the calls of Frostmourne and The Lich King drive me to the Frozen Throne in Icecrown and merged with the true Lich King and became ruler of the Scourge.
